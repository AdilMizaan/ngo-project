Astitva: A Woman's Journey Towards Self-Empowerment

Launched in 2009 by Navratan Foundations, Astitva is our flagship initiative dedicated to breaking this cycle. We reach out to marginalized women in villages, slums, and low-income communities across Delhi/NCR, providing them with free vocational training in tailoring and stitching — transforming them from dependent homemakers to confident, financially independent earners.

What We Teach

Basic Stitching

Introduction to sewing machines, basic hand & machine stitches, threading, and simple garment construction.

Advanced Tailoring

Pattern making, garment fitting, advanced stitching techniques, alterations, and professional finishing.

Fashion Design Basics

Sketching, color theory, fabric selection, trend understanding, and basic design concepts.

Financial Literacy

Budgeting, saving, basic banking, loan awareness, entrepreneurship basics, and income management.

Soft Skills & Entrepreneurship

Communication, confidence building, time management, customer handling, and starting small business ideas.
